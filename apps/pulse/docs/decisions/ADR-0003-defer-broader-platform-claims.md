# ADR-0003: Defer Broader Platform Claims To Proposals

Status: accepted

## Context

Some older Pulse material reflects a broader future direction than the current shipped product. That thinking is still valuable, but mixing it with canonical docs creates confusion.

## Decision

Keep broader future expansion ideas in `docs/proposals/` or `docs/archive/`, not in canonical product docs.

## Consequences

- future ideas are preserved without pretending to be current scope
- onboarding becomes clearer
- product docs become more trustworthy
